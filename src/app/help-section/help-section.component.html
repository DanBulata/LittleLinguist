Help Section!

Welcome to Our English Learning App!

Our app is designed to help Hebrew speakers learn English in an easy and fun way. Whether you are a beginner or looking to improve your skills, our app provides the tools and resources you need to succeed. You will find lessons on vocabulary, grammar, pronunciation, and conversation practice to help you become fluent in English.

Why Learn English?

English is one of the most widely spoken languages in the world. Learning English can open up new opportunities for travel, work, and connecting with people from different cultures. It is also a valuable skill that can help you in your studies and career.

Stay Motivated!

Learning a new language can be challenging, but don't give up! Practice a little every day, and you will see progress. Our app offers interactive lessons and exercises to keep you engaged and motivated. Remember, every step you take brings you closer to your goal of speaking English confidently.

Keep Going!

Consistency is key. Set aside time each day to use the app and practice your English skills. Celebrate your achievements, no matter how small, and keep pushing yourself to improve. With dedication and effort, you can achieve your English learning goals!

Word Sorting Game Instructions

Objective:

Identify which words belong to the selected category and which do not.

How to Play:

Select a Category:

Choose the category you want to learn from the available options (e.g., Fruits, Animals, Colors).

Review the Words:

You will see a list of words. Some words will belong to the selected category, and others will not.

Sort the Words:

For each word, decide if it belongs to the chosen category or not. Click on the appropriate button to categorize each word: "Yes" if the word belongs to the category. "No" if the word does not belong to the category.

Submit Your Answers:

After categorizing all the words, click the "Submit" button to check your answers. You'll receive feedback on whether your choices were correct.

Scoring:

Points are awarded for each correct categorization. Aim for a high score by correctly identifying words in the category.

Game End:

The game continues until you've sorted all the words. Your final score will be displayed at the end of the game.

Tips:

Pay attention to the words and their meanings to make accurate decisions. Familiarize yourself with the category to improve your categorization skills.

Mixed Words Game Instructions

Objective:

Rearrange the scrambled letters of the English word to match the Hebrew word provided.

How to Play:

View the Hebrew Word:

A Hebrew word from the selected category will be displayed on the screen.

Unscramble the English Letters:

Below the Hebrew word, you will see the letters of the English equivalent word, but they are scrambled. Your task is to arrange these letters into the correct English word.

Arrange the Letters:

Drag and drop or click on the letters to reorder them. Continue rearranging until you believe you have the correct spelling.

Submit Your Answer:

Once you think the letters are in the right order, click the "Submit" button. You will receive immediate feedback on whether your answer is correct.

Hints (Optional):

If you are stuck, use a hint to reveal one or more letters in the correct position.

Scoring:

Points are awarded for each correctly unscrambled word. Bonus points for completing words quickly and without using hints.

Game End:

The game continues until you have unscrambled all the words in the category. Your final score will be displayed at the end of the game.

Tips:

Think about common English word patterns. Use your knowledge of the Hebrew word to help unscramble the letters. Have fun unscrambling and learning new words!

Enjoy the game and improve your vocabulary!
